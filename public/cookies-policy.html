Cookies Policy

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit our website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and provide information to website owners.

2. How We Use Cookies

Grey Entropy Research uses cookies to:

  • Remember your preferences and settings
  • Understand how you use our website
  • Improve your browsing experience
  • Provide relevant content and advertisements
  • Analyze website traffic and user behavior

3. Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly. They enable basic functions like page navigation and access to secure areas of the website.

Analytics Cookies

These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our website by collecting and reporting information anonymously.

Functional Cookies

These cookies enable the website to provide enhanced functionality and personalization, such as remembering your preferences.

Marketing Cookies

These cookies are used to track visitors across websites to display relevant and engaging advertisements.

4. Cookie Details

Cookie Name Purpose Duration Type

_analytics Website usage analytics 2 years Analytics

5. Third-Party Cookies

We may also use third-party services that place cookies on your device, including:

  • Google Analytics: For website analytics and performance monitoring
  • Social Media Platforms: For social sharing functionality
  • Content Delivery Networks: For faster content loading

6. Managing Your Cookie Preferences

You can control and manage cookies in several ways:

  • Browser Settings: Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies
  • Opt-out Tools: Use industry opt-out tools for advertising cookies
  • Website Settings: Use our cookie preference center (if available)

7. Browser-Specific Instructions

  • Chrome: Settings > Privacy and Security > Cookies and other site data
  • Firefox: Options > Privacy & Security > Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences > Privacy > Manage Website Data
  • Edge: Settings > Cookies and site permissions

8. Impact of Disabling Cookies

Please note that disabling certain cookies may impact your browsing experience and some features of our website may not function properly.

9. Mobile Devices

On mobile devices, you can manage cookies through your device's browser settings or through the device's privacy settings for app-based browsing.

10. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookies Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal compliance. Please check this page periodically for updates.

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions about our use of cookies or this Cookies Policy, please contact us through our main website.
